Character.AI is a conversational AI platform where the chatbots are built around specific personalities or "characters." Unlike a general-purpose AI like ChatGPT, each bot is designed to stay in character, allowing users to have immersive conversations, role-play scenarios, brainstorm ideas with a specific persona, or practice conversations. Users can also easily create and share their own AI characters.
